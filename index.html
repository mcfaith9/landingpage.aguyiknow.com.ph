A GUY I KNOW – CEBU, INC. was established in 2015. Back then, we were a group of freelancers working together to deliver graphics, animations, and e-Learning outputs to Australia-based clients.

With our inevitable success, we have grown and became an established company. We are skilled in hands-on development of interactive content using a multitude of industry standard tools. These include EnableNow (which includes WebAssist), Articulate 360, Camtasia, Adobe Captivate, Ancile Prodpak, Multiple LMS (Learning Management systems), and the full Adobe Design Suite.
